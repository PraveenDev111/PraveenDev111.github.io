Manifesto

We integrate, collaborate and Challenge

Integrate

Globalization does not necessarily mean Westernisation. Our cross-border service not only connects dots but integrates across East and West, local and universal, physical and digital.

Collaborate

We strive for collective creativity, which is in essence the antithesis of competitive individualism. We first design a relationship where people with different values and positions can work together for a common goal.

Challenge

By bringing out the creator in each of us, we believe in an inclusive creative world. We start where others stop, we stand against inequality, question the norm, and get our hands dirty to create positive change.
